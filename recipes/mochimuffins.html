Chewy Hawaiian Butter Mochi Muffins

Description

Try this Hawaiian butter mochi recipe for a chewy, buttery, fudgey dessert with crisp, crunchy edges. A Hawaiian favorite, this butter mochi is highly addictive!

Ingredients

Dry Ingredients:

  • 3 cups Mochiko sweet rice flour
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • pinch salt

Wet Ingredients:

  • 13.5 oz lite coconut milk
  • 1 ½ cup water
  • 7 Tablespoons butter - melted
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the dry ingredients in a bowl and whisk out any lumps. In a separate bowl, combine all wet ingredients in a bowl and whisk until smooth.
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and whisk together until smooth.
  4. Pour the mixture into a well greased nonstick muffin pan or mini loaf pan and bake for 40 - 45 minutes until the edges and surface turn a golden brown. If baking in a 9 x 9 baking dish, bake for 1 hour.
  5. Remove them from the pan and set them on a wire rack to cool before serving. Be sure to let them cool completely to firm up and get a chewy mochi texture.
